About Us

Executive Summary

Auspicious Elites (AE) is a predominately female run, 100% owned business. The company is primarily a marketing and events company with plans aimed at providing young individuals with opportunities to use their skills of expertise, thus providing them not only with experience in a work environment but also further opportunities to explore the work force as well as other avenues that they may come across.

Further AE aims to create opportunities of development amongst the youth through out outreach programs. These programs will span from skills development, career guidance to psychosocial support. AE will render multiple services such as marketing, event planning/coordinating, consulting on client concepts and assisting with bringing them to fruition and giving guidance, for example assisting a client with getting their clothing line off the ground, their book published or how to make social media work for them to promote their product.

It is common in South Africa to have a business that focuses on youth unemployment issues, but what sets AE apart from the rest is the fact that we are not only looking specifically at youth unemployment but we are also looking at psychosocial problems faced by South African youths in general. This company aims to uplift the youth by building them up from a young age, proving to them that it Is possible to be more that what the stereotypes make you out to be. We aim to encourage young individuals to continue pushing through the struggles that they face because with hard work, nothing is impossible to achieve.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the company of choice for quality clients and to execute the very best integrated marketing, consulting and publishing solutions for them, we also want to provide our clients with resultorientated advertising and marketing strategies which are designed to meet our clients’ expectations.In addition, we aim to ensure that our clients events are executed in accordance to their vision and expectations.

Our Mission

Our mission as AE is to help our clients to develop a clear vision of what they wish to achieve, and to create and effectuate a complete solution that brings their vision into reality and beyond by making use of innovative ideas and strategic plans.We also strive to ensure client satisfaction, creating customer relationships and loyalty by meeting our clients needs and fulfilling their objectives and goals effectively and efficiently. While all the while giving attention social needs and issues identified within the community.

Objectives:

  • Consistent, accurate fulfillment of the client's wishes.
  • Competitive pricing for quality of services offered.
  • Grow market share.
  • Start a community outreach program.
  • Maintain client satisfaction.

Basic goals of AE:

  • Provide opportunities to young individuals of all races seeking work experience through our various avenues, including but not limited to hiring them as employees, giving them work experience and allowing them the freedom to venture out into the corporate world on their own. As well as partnering with other upstart businesses and involving them in events planned, allowing their brand to be showcased.
  • Providing communities with outreach programs specified at youth, that will develop skills targeted to increase their employability, including but limited to computer training, classes on CV writing or interpersonal skills on how to respond and carry one self in an interview.
  • Provide entrepreneurs an opportunity to market themselves and their work through us and our various channels of communication with the public (specifically social media and events).
  • Develop a strong client base.
  • Use social media (twitter specifically) to employ at least 10% of youth through this avenue. Board of directors of AE are youthful and have recognized a need for social media in today's age but also the necessity of using it as a marketing tool and recruiting tool, on a daily basis multiple youths seek employment through retweets or advertise their business or talent on twitter. By hosting events and partnering with the youth, AE can host an event featured with a show case of products of one client, decorating the venue with artwork from one youth and have a night filled with entertainment from another youth found on social media. Giving youths exposure and paying them as well.
