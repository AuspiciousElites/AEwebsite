List of Books

  • Animalistic
  • Jade Novelist
Animalistic is an anthology of poetry written by Jade Novelist. The anthology contains 16 poems titled using 16 different animals. The poems focus on various topics from dealing with enemies to loving your own space. All the poems contain Zoomorphism and Jade uses it to explain and reflect human behavior.

  • Letters To Past Loves
  • Jade Novelist x Maria Wolfe
Letters to Past Loves is equally a celebration and what one may choose to call a cremation of love lost and love delayed. Penned by hearts who have been both heart breakers and heartbroken. Undelivered letters hoping to find their way from the bottom of the spam box. To love a writer is to be a muse, and to love a poet is to be recreated into confessional stanzas and stories in your honour, however to love a poet means to have your story told in a variety of renditions, some of which may be nails to your coffin, and some a bouquet of dead flowers, and on some days the truest form of love and appreciation.


"Love is like a letter wrote Life is like an envelope Be careful who you give it to They might not give it back to you" - Matt Corby, Letters
  • BLVCK Ink
  • Jade Novelist x Maria Wolfe
BLVCK Ink is the second book from the BLVCK series. Jade Novelist and Maira Wolfe decided to invite new writers from all over Africa to write with them in BLVCK Ink. The main concept behind BLVCK Ink is to accentuate on the diverse experiences these new African writers have gone through because of being black and these experiences are shared through poetry. Although BLVCK Ink may highlight the hardships black people endure, it also acknowledges and praises those who won against the odds. Furthermore, although black people are tagged with negative qualities, Ink highlights the good deeds done by us the daughters and sons of the golden brown soil of Africa.

  • BLVCK Ink - The Exhibition (Bonus)
  • Jade Novelist x Maira Wolfe
BLVCK Exhibition a surprise release by Jade Novelist and Maira Wolfe, it is made up of all the artwork used in the BLVCK book and BLVCK Ink, and narrations compiled by Maira for all the BLVCK series poems. However, like in BLVCK Ink, there is a special twist as it also contains 10 exclusive poems written by Jade Novelist.

  • The BLVCK Book (Standard Édition)
  • Jade Novelist x Maira Wolfe
The BLVCK book is an opus written by two authors namely, Jade Novelist and Maira Wolfe. It contains 20 collaborative poems. The book focuses mostly on accentuating and confronting problems faced by black people on daily basis. These vary from having to prove their worth to individuals whom belittle them, to ensuring that they pay back all those who have supported them through all the hardships. In addition, the book tries to make us aware of how we suffer in the hands of each other rather than supporting one another

  • Beautiful Imperfections (Luna Words)
  • Jade Novelist x Maira Wolfe
A book written by Jade Novelist and Maira Wolfe, about two married individuals who fall in love with one another and decide to leave their respective spouses to be together. A story told through poetry and shorties
